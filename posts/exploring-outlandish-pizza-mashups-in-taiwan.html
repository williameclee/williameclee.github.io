Cuisine Fusion and Cultural Politics

Exploring Outlandish Pizza Mashups in Taiwan

Italians are proud of their pizza, and protect the art of making Neapolitan pizza as a cultural heritage [1]. As a consequence, few dishes stir up more controversy than the pineapple Hawaiian pizza: some criticise it as cultural appreciation, and Italy’s ambassadors in the UK were offended by such an abomination [2, 3]. On the other hand, many people love it, and Iceland’s president had to officially apologise for wishing he could ban it [4].
